      How to Play!

    1. Sign Up/Log In to start the Quiz.

    2. All questions are multiple choice, with 4 possible answers presented.

    3. Make sure to read each question carefully, and then select your chosen answer by clicking the relevant answer box.

    4. For each correct answer you will earn 10 points, but watch out as each wrong answer you will loose 10 points! The quiz will also be timed giving the chance to earn time booster points once you answer all questions.

    5. Submit your score at the end to see where you place on the leaderboard!
